My Family Background

Hello everyone. Introducing, my big family. My soul, my half, my safe place where i found comfort when i needed.

I come from family of 9, which 7 siblings in total. Among those, i have 2 brothers and 5 sisters. My ayah, passed away in 2023 (Al-Fatihah). After that, my mum carry all of us together, with everything completed. Me and my siblings had a very strong love-hate siblings relationship. Sometimes we fight, we admire each other, and sometimes, we held each other's hand when we needed. Through my whole life, i grew up watching my siblings getting married, one by one, then they had their own children, which where i found my inner child in them. I admire my nieces and nephews more than anyone could ever known. They are my first bestfriend, as i had no other younger sibling. I spent most of my time with them, live love and laugh all together....
I hope they know how much i love them.
